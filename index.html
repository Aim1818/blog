Welcome to My Blog

This blog documents our BSCS Industrial Tour 2025, highlighting the companies we visited and the insights we gained from each speaker.

Visited Companies

June 9, 2025

Foundever Philippines

Location: Bonifacio Global City, Taguig

Speakers: Ms. Zoe Diaz de Rivera (IBPAP Representative) and Mr. Alman Manzano (Regional IT Director of Foundever)

Reflection: At Foundever, we learned about the real-world impact of customer service and business process outsourcing. The speakers emphasized the critical role of IT in BPO operations and the importance of nurturing digital talent in the Philippines.

Google Philippines

Location: Bonifacio Global City, Taguig

Speaker: Atty. Yves Gonzalez (Head of Government Affairs and Public Policy)

Reflection: Visiting Google was a dream come true. Atty. Gonzalez talked about innovation, policy, and how technology influences public governance. We gained new perspectives on how responsible tech can create large-scale societal impact.

June 10, 2025

Teleperformance Philippines

Location: Mandaluyong City

Speaker: Ms. Beatrice Grace Bonjibod (Marketing Specialist)

Reflection: At Teleperformance, we explored the importance of tech infrastructure and digital branding. Ms. Bonjibod also highlighted how innovation and empathy help create powerful customer experiences.

Kollab Philippines

Location: Makati City

Speaker: Mr. Ralph Vincent Regalado (Chief Scientist & Head of AI)

Reflection: Kollab introduced us to the power of artificial intelligence in team collaboration. Mr. Regalado shared how they’re building smart tools using AI, giving us a glimpse into practical, local applications of advanced tech.

June 11, 2025

Asian Development Bank (ADB)

Location: Ortigas, Pasig City

Speaker: Mr. Matt Farley (Director of Infrastructure and Technology Division)

Reflection: At ADB, Mr. Farley talked about digital transformation across Asia. He emphasized the need for inclusive tech infrastructure and inspired us to think about how technology can drive social and environmental progress.

Microsourcing

Location: Eastwood, Quezon City

Speaker: Ms. Haidee Enriquez (CEO of Microsourcing)

Reflection: Our visit to Microsourcing gave us insight into the future of managed outsourcing. Ms. Enriquez discussed leadership, global talent, and how Filipino professionals are rising as key players in the international tech space.
