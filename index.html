Welcome to My Blog

This blog documents our BSCS Industrial Tour 2025, highlighting the companies we visited and the insights we gained from each speaker.

Visited Companies

June 9, 2025

Foundever Philippines

Location: Bonifacio Global City, Taguig

Reflection: At Foundever, we learned about the real-world impact of customer service and business process outsourcing. The speaker highlighted how communication, empathy, and tech skills are essential in today's global service industry.

Google Philippines

Location: Bonifacio Global City, Taguig

Reflection: Visiting Google was a dream come true. The speaker discussed innovation, workplace culture, and how Google builds products that solve real-world problems. We also learned about career opportunities and their support for developer communities in the Philippines.

June 10, 2025

Teleperformance Philippines

Location: Mandaluyong City

Reflection: At Teleperformance, we explored the importance of tech infrastructure behind call center operations. We learned how data security, customer experience, and AI-driven analytics work together in the BPO ecosystem.

Kollab Philippines

Location: Makati City

Reflection: Kollab introduced us to the startup mindset. The speaker talked about collaboration tools, remote work trends, and how agility and innovation fuel their team. It gave us a glimpse into the future of digital teamwork.

June 11, 2025

Asian Development Bank (ADB)

Location: Ortigas, Pasig City

Reflection: At ADB, we learned about tech for social good. The speaker emphasized how the bank funds climate-resilient infrastructure and digital transformation in Asia. It was inspiring to see how computer science can support global development goals.

Microsourcing

Location: Eastwood, Quezon City

Reflection: Our visit to Microsourcing showcased the rise of outsourcing for IT and creative services. The speaker stressed the demand for Filipino tech talent and how freelancers and specialists are supported through managed teams.
